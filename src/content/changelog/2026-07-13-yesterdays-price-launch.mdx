Second product is live at [yesterdaysprice.com](https://yesterdaysprice.com).

Guess what an everyday item cost in a past year — a gallon of gas in 1987, a dozen eggs in 1969 — in six tries, then share your result. Everyone plays the same puzzle, and a new one drops every morning.

It's a fully static site with zero marginal cost per player: every puzzle is pre-computed at build time from public BLS price data, and streaks live in your browser. No database, no accounts.
