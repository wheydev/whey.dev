Fourth product is live at [playfanride.com](https://playfanride.com).

Pick your American football team and FanRide turns its history into a mountain stage — the highs and lows rendered as a 3D ride you climb season by season. At the summit you meet your Misery Score: one number for how much your fandom has hurt.

The scale is fixed, so a score means the same thing for every fan base. Share your card, duel a rival fan, and settle who's actually suffered more.
